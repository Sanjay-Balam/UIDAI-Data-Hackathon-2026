3. Methodology

3.1 Data Cleaning Pipeline

Challenges Identified:

  • State name variations (50+ variations ‚Üí normalized to 36 official names)
  • District duplicates (Bengaluru/Bangalore, 24 Parganas variations)
  • Garbage data ("100000", "?", "5th cross" as district names)

Solution: Created cleaning_utils.py with normalization functions

3.2 Analysis Methods

Pillar Formula Interpretation
SAI (Service Pressure) SPS = Total Transactions / Active PIN Codes High SPS = Overloaded service points
CLCS (Child Compliance) Z = (District - Mean) / Std Dev Z < -1.5 = HIGH RISK
DIH (Seasonality) Monthly volume analysis June-Aug = School Rush
